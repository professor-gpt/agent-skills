# CRM Guidelines

## Entry Requirements
- **Lead Information**: Capture accurate contact details and engagement history.
- **Opportunity Stage**: Reflect current status and next steps.
- **Data Validation**: Cross-reference all entries with trusted sources before submission.

## Best Practices
- Regularly update CRM with latest client interactions.
- Avoid speculative entries; base inputs on verifiable information.
- Ensure changes in CRM are documented with timestamps and author info.

## Review Process
- Review CRM entries weekly for completeness and accuracy.
- Flag any discrepancies for further investigation before processing deals.
